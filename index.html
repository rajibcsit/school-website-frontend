Welcome To Mirpur M.L High School, Sadullapur,Gaibandha.

It gives us immense pleasure to declare that Mirpur High School, Sadullapur, Gaibandha has entered into a new horizon of digital Bangladesh. This school a part of history and tradition has unveiled the screen not only of the website but also a college Management information. Now all information about administration, examination, academic activities, games and sports and teachers will be available in our website. Our aim is to ensure current information regarding our college. I believe that a dynamic, transparent and efficient education management information system will be developed farther by using this automation. All the Departments of this college already arranged some multimedia class rooms. I make it sure that all the classes will be taken by using multimedia within the shortest possible time. Let us sacrifice our all comforts for the welfare of our student, college and country. I firmly believe that this automation system will help our college to move forward in all respect. I convey my heartfelt thanks to all who have spent their valuable time and efforts to run this system.
